Player X's Turn

Instructions

Info

This is a tictactoe game that works slightly different than most. This game idea was introduced to us by our friend and we decided to create a website where we could easily play it.

In this game, there exists a master tictactoe board with smaller boards within each position of the master board. If your confused, just follow the colored square to see where you can play next.

How to play

You need 2 players, X and O The first player picks any position in any square The next player must play in the position in the masterboard corresponding to the square of the smaller board that was clicked First to 3 in a row in a smaller board wins that position in the master board First to 3 in a row in the master board wins
